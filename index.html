About Prepress Automation

My name is Koen Van de Poel and I used to work at Agfa Graphics (now Eco3) until recently, mainly on automation for Prepress (Apogee), integrations (usually using JDF) and digital printing.

I am responsible for the prepress specification documents within the CIP4 (JDF) organization and received the CIP4 fellowship in 2012.

Supported languages: Dutch, English, French and German.
